A Little About Me

Where I'm from

I grew up in the Washington, DC area, moved to Chicago for college, and now call Madison, WI home.

My Interests

I play music, currently in the band Gentle Brontosaurus, and previously in TL;DR. I play keyboard and pocket trumpet, and I'm halfway decent on electric bass or guitar.

I read and write speculative fiction, and regularly participate in NaNoWriMo. So basically, even when I'm not programming, you can generally find me at some type of keyboard.

My other hobbies include board games, video games, biking, and skiing.

Education and Experience

At the University of Chicago, I majored in physics, and also made time for Russian language and literature. In human languages, I also studied Latin for many years, and conversational Spanish and German.

In terms of programming languages, I started my journey early on, teaching myself BASIC, and enough HTML to put together a Geocities page. In highschool, I learned a little Kobol, ForTran, and Visual Basic. In college, I learned Python. And then working in a technical services role at Epic, I learned Cache/M, more Visual Basic, and a bit SQL and Perl. Now with DBC, I'm learning more HTML and CSS, Ruby, and much more!
